Pravin Kumar

Assistant Professor, Sri Balaji University, Pune · Ph.D. Scholar, CVBL, IIIT Allahabad

I’m an Assistant Professor at Sri Balaji University, Pune and a Ph.D. scholar at CVBL, IIIT-Allahabad working on medical image analysis — how deep learning can make diabetic retinopathy screening accurate, interpretable, and reliable across datasets. My recent work spans lesion-anchored multi-view transformers for cross-dataset DR grading, multi-scale dual-attention networks for microaneurysm segmentation, and lesion-graph ordinal reasoning with calibrated deferral for DR triage.

I teach Design and Analysis of Algorithms and Pattern Recognition & Deep Learning, and serve as University SPOC for the Smart India Hackathon. Previously, I taught at Galgotias University and worked as a Data Scientist building face-recognition and YOLO-based PPE-compliance systems at Ispeck Digital Solutions.

Always open to research collaborations or a good conversation about computer vision, medical imaging, or reliable AI for healthcare — feel free to reach out. I also run CSwithPravin, a digital education initiative.
