Pravin Kumar

Assistant Professor · Ph.D. Scholar, CVBL, IIIT Allahabad

I am an Assistant Professor at Sri Balaji University, Pune and a Ph.D. scholar at the Computer Vision and Biometrics Lab (CVBL), IIIT Allahabad, working on medical image analysis, deep learning, and computer vision.

My research focuses on diabetic retinopathy screening — lesion segmentation, fundus image analysis, cross-dataset grading, interpretable AI, transformers, and reliable AI for healthcare. I also teach algorithms, data structures, Python programming, pattern recognition, and deep learning.

Previously, I worked as a Data Scientist building computer-vision solutions for face recognition and PPE-compliance monitoring, and I run the digital education channel CSwithPravin.
